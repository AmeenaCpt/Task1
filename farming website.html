We farm with both organic and conventional techniques and strive to produce food and fiber in the most
ethical and environmentally friendly ways possible. We also manage habitat areas that are key resources for
our local ecosystem as well as for migrating waterfowl. For us at Bowles farming company, “sustainability”
is part of everything we do. As a farming family with over 16 years of history, sustainability is not just
a catch phrase for us, it is a way of life.
